Top picks of the week!!

Ghost Beach by R.L. Stine follows siblings Jerry and Terri as they uncover a mysterious ghost story while visiting relatives near an eerie beach. The tale blends suspense and twists, as they must determine who to trust in a haunting conflict between the living and the dead.

The Jungle Omnibus by Ruskin Bond is a collection of stories that capture the beauty, mystery, and vitality of the Indian wilderness. Through vivid descriptions and heartfelt narratives, Bond immerses readers in the lives of animals, people, and the landscapes of the jungle.

"The Communication Book" by Mikael Krogerus and Roman Tschäppeler explores key principles and tools for effective communication, offering practical insights into improving interpersonal interactions. It presents concepts in a concise and engaging manner, making complex ideas accessible and actionable.
